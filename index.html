The Central Region's Premier Community Supported Agriculture

Support Local Farmers

Our farmers support sustainable and restorative farming practices. Farmers market to CSA customers earlier in the season, allowing them to focus on farming during the busier seasons. The cash flow is spread throughout the season, instead of being concentration during harvest time only.
